About CareerVista

Making Career Decisions Clear and Confident

Students often choose careers based on trends, peer influence, or incomplete information — leading to skill mismatches and uncertainty about job readiness. CareerVista changes that.

Our platform helps students evaluate multiple career paths using employability insights, skill gap identification, preparation timelines, and market trends.

By combining career simulation with guided learning, CareerVista empowers students to understand exactly where they stand and what they need to do next.
